mutombo mutombo , a shot-blocking congolese centre whose legendary finger-wagging motion became one of the most recognized gestures in the game , was a four-times nba defensive player of the year and led the league in blocked shots for five straight seasons .
mutombo played in the nba from 1991 until 2009 , recording 11,729 points , 12,359 rebounds , and 3,289 blocks .
