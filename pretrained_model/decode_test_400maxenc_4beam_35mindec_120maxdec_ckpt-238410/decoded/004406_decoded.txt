the eshima ohashi bridge in japan is the third largest of its kind in the world .
the concrete road bridge stretches 1.7 km or just over 1 mile long and has a width of 6.1 per cent on the shimane prefecture side and 5.1 per cent on the tottori prefecture side .
the tallest concrete pylon on the millau viaduct is 800ft on its own - higher than most buildings .
